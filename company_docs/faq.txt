Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What are your business hours?
A: Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status by logging into your account on our website and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we will send you a password reset link. The link expires in 24 hours.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International shipping takes 7-14 business days. Shipping costs vary by location and are calculated at checkout.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings page. Go to Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can reach us through this chat, email us at support@company.com, or call us at 1-800-555-0123 during business hours.
