Shipping Information

Standard Shipping:
- Delivery within 5-7 business days.
- Free for orders over $50.
- $4.99 flat rate for orders under $50.

Express Shipping:
- Delivery within 2-3 business days.
- Costs $12.99 regardless of order size.

Overnight Shipping:
- Next business day delivery.
- Costs $24.99.
- Orders must be placed before 2 PM EST.

International Shipping:
- Available to 50+ countries.
- Delivery within 7-14 business days.
- Costs calculated based on weight and destination.
- Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.

Order Processing:
- Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
- You will receive a confirmation email with tracking details once your order ships.
- During peak seasons (Black Friday, holidays), processing may take up to 3 business days.
