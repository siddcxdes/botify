Account Management

Creating an Account:
- Visit our website and click "Sign Up".
- Enter your name, email, and create a password.
- Verify your email by clicking the link we send you.
- Your account is ready to use!

Updating Your Profile:
- Log in and go to Settings > Profile.
- You can update your name, email, phone number, and shipping address.
- Changes are saved automatically.

Deleting Your Account:
- If you wish to delete your account, please email support@company.com.
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
- All your order history and data will be removed within 30 days.
- Any active subscriptions will be cancelled.

Security:
- We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) from Settings > Security.
- Never share your password with anyone.
- If you suspect unauthorized access, reset your password immediately and contact support.
