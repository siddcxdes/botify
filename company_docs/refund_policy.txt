Refund Policy

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase. To request a refund, please email support@company.com with your order number.

Conditions for refund:
- The product must be unused and in original packaging.
- Digital products are non-refundable once downloaded.
- Subscription refunds are prorated based on remaining days.
- Refund processing takes 5-7 business days.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

If your order arrived damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We will send a replacement at no extra cost.

For orders over $500, a restocking fee of 10% may apply.
