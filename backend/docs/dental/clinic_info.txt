ORAL HEALTH TIPS FROM OUR DOCTORS
1. Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste for at least 2 minutes
2. Floss once a day to prevent gum disease
3. Visit a dentist every 6 months for routine check-ups
4. Limit sugary and acidic foods — they erode enamel
5. Replace your toothbrush every 3 months
6. Don't ignore bleeding gums — it could be early gum disease
7. Wear a mouthguard if you grind your teeth at night
8. Professional cleaning removes tartar that brushing can't
