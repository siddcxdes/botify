PRACTICE AREAS

1. Corporate Law
   Our corporate law division assists businesses of all sizes with company formation, mergers and acquisitions, shareholder agreements, compliance audits, and corporate governance. Lead Partner: Adv. Raghav Mehta (15+ years experience).

2. Family Law
   We handle divorce proceedings, child custody disputes, alimony negotiations, adoption cases, and domestic violence protection orders with sensitivity and discretion. Lead Partner: Adv. Priya Sharma (12+ years experience).

3. Criminal Defense
   From white-collar crimes to serious felony charges, our criminal defense team provides aggressive representation at every stage — from bail hearings to trial and appeals. Lead Partner: Adv. Karan Singh (18+ years experience).

4. Intellectual Property
   We protect your creative and business assets through trademark registration, patent filing, copyright protection, IP litigation, and licensing agreements.

5. Contract Disputes
   Our team resolves commercial contract breaches, vendor disputes, non-compete violations, and partnership disagreements through negotiation, arbitration, or litigation.

6. Real Estate Law
   We assist with property title verification, sale deed drafting, landlord-tenant disputes, and RERA compliance matters.
